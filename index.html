Who We Are

Academi is a premier American private security company, renowned for its operational excellence, advanced training capabilities, and strategic global presence. Founded in 1997, Academi became widely known for its work in high-risk environments and has since evolved into a key part of the Constellis group — a leading provider of risk management and mission support services worldwide.

With decades of experience supporting U.S. government agencies, international organizations, and private sector clients, Academi offers tailored solutions in protective security, training, logistics, and crisis response.
