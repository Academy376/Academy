Securing Success at Home and Abroad

In times of geo-political uncertainty, governments and commercial organizations around the world require specialized support capabilities to facilitate their critical operations. With cross-sector partnerships and expertise in multiagency coordination, Constellis draws on the depth of our program and risk management experience to help our customers conduct business safely and efficiently no matter where they are in the world. Our comprehensive and integrated solutions span national security, domestic and international operations, training and global support services.

Armed and Unarmed Physical Security Services for Government and Commercial Customers

Personnel, facilities, data, networks, equipment and other assets are vulnerable to a number of attacks. Constellis is a trusted and experienced provider of security services. Our integrated security solutions range from uniformed protective services to high threat protection for government, corporate and nongovernmental customers. We deliver quality-driven, professional services to safeguard personnel, infrastructure, and other valuable assets and investments.

Safeguarding Critical National Installations from Nuclear Terrorism

Constellis works directly with the U.S. Federal Government and commercial entities to safeguard our most critical assets and facilities, in support of the Department of Energy (DoE), Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC), National Aeronautical and Space Administration (NASA), and other national strategic assets and resources.

Comprehensive Risk Management Solutions to Protect Your Organization

Academi provides professional risk management services from Fortune 500 companies to small businesses to help organizations prevent threats and eliminate vulnerabilities that reduce risks while enhancing organizational resilience. Constellis provides a comprehensive set of assessment and training services that is tailored to meet the security requirements of any organization. We provide thorough risk assessments to identify physical and operational vulnerabilities, and then develop integrated security programs with detailed contingency plans. Our specialists reduce risk from an “all hazards” perspective—analyzing your security conditions from multiple angles. Additionally, we work closely with our customers to allocate appropriate security resources and offer continuous assessment and customized training.

Special Event Staffing for Heightened Security

From major sporting events to national political conventions, advanced planning and a strong security presence is necessary for ensuring a safe environment for its VIPs and attendees. Constellis specializes in security services for various public, private and U.S. Government events. This includes advanced threat assessment, planning and monitoring, large crowd control, and immediate crisis response. We staff special events, after-hours events, and augment existing staff during heightened security periods. Who We Are

Academi is a premier American private security company, renowned for its operational excellence, advanced training capabilities, and strategic global presence. Founded in 1997, Academi became widely known for its work in high-risk environments and has since evolved into a key part of the Constellis group — a leading provider of risk management and mission support services worldwide. With decades of experience supporting U.S. government agencies, international organizations, and private sector clients, Academi offers tailored solutions in protective security, training, logistics, and crisis response.
