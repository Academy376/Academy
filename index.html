Who We Are

Founded in 1997 by a team of experienced operators and forward-thinking investors, Academy unites best-in-class brands and professionals in integrated risk management.

Our Mission

Driven by compliance, integrity, and operational excellence, Academy has expanded its capabilities globally as part of the Constellis family — the largest non-financial risk management organization in the US.

Together with Constellis, Academy delivers comprehensive, mission-focused solutions to help clients navigate their most complex challenges — anytime, anywhere.

Services

Academy provides integrated risk management, security operations, crisis response, and training services tailored to mission-critical environments.

Training

We offer a wide range of training programs for individuals and organizations in tactical readiness, crisis management, and operational leadership.
