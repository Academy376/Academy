Who We Are

Founded in 1997 by a team of experienced operators and forward-thinking investors, Academy was established with the goal of uniting best-in-class brands, assets, and professionals in the field of integrated risk management.

Driven by a strong culture of compliance, integrity, and operational excellence, Academy has continually expanded its capabilities and global presence. Today, it operates as part of the Constellis family of companies — the largest and most diversified non-financial risk management organization in the United States.

Together with Constellis, Academy delivers comprehensive, mission-focused solutions to help clients navigate their most complex challenges — anytime, anywhere.

Services

Academy provides integrated risk management, security operations, crisis response, and training services tailored to mission-critical environments.

Training
